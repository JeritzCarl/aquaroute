Data Deletion Request

AquaRoute respects your privacy. If you would like your account and associated data to be deleted, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to aquaroute.support@gmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the email address or phone number linked to your AquaRoute account.
  3. We will process your request and confirm once your data has been removed from our systems.

For questions, please contact us at aquaroute.support@gmail.com.
